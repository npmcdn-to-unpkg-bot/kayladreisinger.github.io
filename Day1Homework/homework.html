Get outside. Get Running. Get high.

More specifically, get a Runner's High.

Runners High is a Colorado based running team. We take on athletes who aim to compete and win in ultramarathons. Our coaches have a combined 100 years of experience. Their passion for the sport and love of coaching is what drives Runners High.

Our team offers:

  • One-on-One training
  • 24 hour access to yoga studio and free weights gym
  • Team Practices
  • Nutrition Consulting
  • Sunday Fun Runs!

Join us for weekly fun runs on Sundays at 8am. We meet at Coors Brewery and carpool to a trailhead. Please note that this run is open to runners of all levels, we would love to meet you and foster your athletic drive.

Contact us to meet the team and participate in a practice!
