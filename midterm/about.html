Mission

Our mission at Floating Fields Farm is to educate and empower our community. Growing organic goodness on a sustainable farm is our medium of choice.

Floating Fields Full Time Team
Kyle Newman

The man, the myth, the legend. Yup, Floating Fields farm manager is a bad ass. Originally from Connecticut Kyle has been down south for two years. After years pursuing a formal higher education in medicine, Kyle decided to apply his passions in another direction.

James Clark

One-third farmer, one-third musician, one-third big heart. You can find James leading the pack on the farm with a big smile on his face. Kyle's right-hand man and high school bestie also moved from Connecticut and simply can not get enough of the Texan sunshine.

Matt Tru

A nomad at heart, Matt is always on the road. Most recently his travels led him through California and Oregon before returning home to Texas. Matt applies all of his knowledge and experience to working on the farm in a way that is warm and uplifting.

Jim Beam

Jim has spent the last ten years living off the land in Hawaii. He brings with him a level of expertise unmatched by anyone on the farm. As such, his insight is always sought after and simple demeanour a calming reassurance.
